AI Bottleneck Diagnostic — Research Pipeline

This version generates the diagnosis automatically via an LLM pipeline (no human diagnostician), and runs every candidate sentence through two transparency gates before saving it: an anti-restatement check and an anti-horoscope discrimination check. The original human-diagnostician version is preserved at index.html.

Run a diagnosis

Enter the subject's name and a free-text description of what they said about their AI usage blocker. The pipeline extracts their explicit claims, generates a candidate diagnosis sentence, and checks it against both gates before saving.

Report an outcome

Once a diagnosis has been shown to the subject, record what actually happened. This feeds the recurring outcome loop — real (diagnosis → outcome) data over time, instead of one-off interviews.
